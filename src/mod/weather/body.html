Use this tool to find the latitude and longitude for the weather configuration.

  1. Search for cities or postal codes.
  2. Optionally open map link by clicking on a location in the results and fine tune the coordinates selection by right-clicking on any place on the map and selecting "show address". This will give you the latitude and longitude of the place below your mouse cursor.
  3. Copy over the latitude and longitude into the configurator input fields.
