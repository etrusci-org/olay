Use this tool to find the latitude and longitude for the weather configuration.

  1. Search for a location by typing in the field below.
  2. Copy and paste the latitude and longitude into the configurator input fields.
  3. Optionally open the map link by clicking on a location in the results and fine tune the coordinates selection by right-clicking on any place on the map and selecting "show address" from the context menu. This will give you the latitude and longitude of the place below your mouse cursor.


The search string can be a location name or a postal code.
Minimum 2 characters.
2 characters will only match exact matching locations.
3 and more characters will perform fuzzy matching.
